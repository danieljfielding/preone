Brand Owner Example: Chobani × PreOne × MPAK — 2026

In 2026, Chobani yoghurt tubs collected from kerbside PP programs feed directly into the PreOne SA Hub. rPP is compounded into ASPs for thin-wall injection food containers — closing the loop on Chobani's own packaging. Hot washed, purified, IML de-labelled, colour sorted, FDA certified.
