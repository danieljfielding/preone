The Facility

South Australian
Purification Hub #1

A purpose-built 12,000m² recycling facility on an existing 7-hectare site in South Australia — currently under construction and on track for production in 2026.

Construction Underway

The 12,000m² facility is actively under construction on the Re.Group 7-hectare South Australian site. Steel framework complete and fit-out progressing on schedule.

Food-Grade rPP from 2026

Food-grade recycled polypropylene produced from kerbside-collected household PP containers — including Chobani tubs — hot washed, purified, IML de-labelled and colour sorted. FDA certified (LNO).

What We Produce

Australian Resin
Output Mix

The SA Hub produces a diversified range of recycled polymer resins from kerbside-collected and commercially-recovered plastic streams.

Food & Consumer Grade Rigids

  • rPP — food-grade, from kerbside PP containers incl. Chobani tubs. Priority output #1
  • rHDPE — consumer and industrial grades
  • PET flakes — bottle and tray applications

Hot washed, purified, IML de-labelled, colour sorted. FDA food grade certified (LNO). Eco Modulation ready.

Non-Food Contact Films

  • rLDPE — flexible packaging and film applications
  • rLLDPE — stretch film and agricultural applications

Soft plastics from kerbside and retail drop-off processed through HydroDyn® — removing all contaminants for clean film-grade flake compounding.

Brand Owner Example: Chobani × PreOne × MPAK — 2026

In 2026, Chobani yoghurt tubs collected from kerbside PP programs feed directly into the PreOne SA Hub. rPP is compounded into Application Specific Polymers for thin-wall injection food containers — closing the loop on Chobani's own packaging. Hot washed, purified, IML de-labelled, colour sorted, FDA certified.
