The Linear Economy

Three Barriers
to Circularity

The plastic value chain is dominated by virgin resin producers and fragmented across players with no incentive to collaborate. PreOne removes all three barriers simultaneously.

1

No Coordination Between Waste & Polymers

Limited expertise and coordination between the world of waste and the world of polymers creates circularity barriers that no single player can overcome. MRFs, converters and brand owners each operate in isolation.

2

Virgin Resin Dominates Economics

More than 90% of all plastic produced is polluting the planet. Limited economic equity for circular participants means there is no financial incentive to close the loop.

3

No Single-Pack Recycled Resin Solution

Packaging manufacturers face expensive, risky trial-and-error with inconsistent recycled materials. There is no equivalent to "just add water" — until now. PreOne's ASP resins are fully engineered, drop-in solutions.

PreOne's Answer

We Make It Circular

PreOne solves the global plastics waste problem by creating a circular economy where value is created for all participants — from material recovery organisations through to consumers.

World-Leading Technology

We deploy the HydroDyn® advanced mechanical recycling system — purifying plastics to 100 parts per million at 90m² per second. No other traditional washing system achieves this level of purification.

End-to-End Coordination

We bridge the world of waste to the world of polymers and influence circular participation through equitable partnerships — securing feedstock, producing resin, and delivering to converters and brand owners.

Application Specific Polymers

Our collaborative business model produces sustainable remanufactured resins and packaging for the global market. We engineer the polymer to meet converter, brand owner and consumer needs — all in one pellet.
