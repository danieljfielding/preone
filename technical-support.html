How We Help

Technical Support
Across the Value Chain

PreOne's technical team supports customers at every stage — from initial resin specification through to production optimisation and quality assurance.

Resin Selection

Application-matched polymer specification. We assess your functional requirements, aesthetics, and certification needs to recommend the right ASP grade.

Processing Support

Injection moulding, blow moulding, extrusion and film processing parameters. Melt flow, temperature profiles, cycle times and tooling guidance.

Quality Assurance

Material test reports, certificates of conformity, and FDA/food-grade documentation. Batch traceability from kerbside collection to finished pellet.

Troubleshooting

Remote and on-site support for processing issues. Warpage, colour variation, melt inconsistency — our chemists diagnose and resolve production problems fast.
