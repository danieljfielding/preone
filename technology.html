The Technology

HydroDyn®
Purification System

The HydroDyn® system unfolds film layers and effectively cleans the plastic surface with adjustable friction and highly turbulent water flow — reducing residual contamination to less than 1% at the Pre-Cleaning Spoke, then further purifying to below 100ppm at the Purification Hub.

No other traditional washing system can achieve this level of purification. Currently installed in 16 facilities across Europe and Australia — with a proven track record since 2014.

The Modular Process

Pre-Washing &
Purification

The PreOne system operates in two stages — Pre-Cleaning Spokes located at MRFs, and Purification Hubs where virgin-replacement resins are produced.

1
HydroCleaner
Pre-Cleaning Spoke at the MRF. Residual contamination reduced to <1%. Key technology unfolds film layers with adjustable friction and turbulent water flow.
2
HydroGrind
Two-stage shredding. Process water separated and collected for continued use. Pre-cleaned material shredded to 25mm.
3
HydroPure
Purification Hub stage. Residual contamination reduced to <100ppm — enabling resins for high-value food applications. No other system matches this.
4
AeroPure ONE
Drying and air purification. Residual moisture <3%. Separates flexible films from rigid polymers. Final classification step.

Hub and Spoke Model

Pre-Cleaning Spokes are positioned at MRF sites to achieve large geographical coverage, scale progressively with demand, distribute supply risk and minimise carbon footprint by only transporting clean, usable material. Central Purification Hubs then produce resins in large capacities for converters and brand owners.
