The Technology

HydroDyn®
Purification System

The HydroDyn® system unfolds film layers and effectively cleans the plastic surface with adjustable friction and highly turbulent water flow — reducing residual contamination to less than 1% at the Pre-Cleaning Spoke, then purifying to below 100ppm at the Purification Hub.

No other traditional washing system achieves this level of purification. Currently installed in 16 facilities across Europe and Australia — proven since 2014.

The Modular Process

Pre-Washing &
Purification

Two stages — Pre-Cleaning Spokes at MRFs, and Purification Hubs where virgin-replacement resins are produced.

1
HydroCleaner
Pre-Cleaning Spoke at MRF. Contamination to <1%. Adjustable friction and turbulent water flow unfolds film layers.
2
HydroGrind
Two-stage shredding. Process water separated and collected for reuse. Pre-cleaned material shredded to 25mm.
3
HydroPure
Purification Hub stage. Contamination to <100ppm — enabling food-grade high-value applications. No other system matches this.
4
AeroPure ONE
Final drying and air purification. Moisture <3%. Separates flexible films from rigid polymers. Classified for compounding.

Hub and Spoke Model

Pre-Cleaning Spokes positioned at MRF sites achieve large geographical coverage, scale progressively with demand, distribute supply risk and minimise carbon footprint — only clean, usable material is transported. Central Purification Hubs then produce resins in large capacities.
